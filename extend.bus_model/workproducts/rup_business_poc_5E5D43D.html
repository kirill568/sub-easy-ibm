Артефакт: Business Architectural Proof-of-Concept
This artifact is a solution, which may simply be conceptual, to the architecturally-significant business requirements.
Домены: Business Modeling
Типы рабочих продуктов: Концепция
Назначение

The purpose of the Business Architectural Proof-of-Concept is to determine whether there exists, or is likely to exist, a solution (or a set of solutions) that satisfies the architecturally-significant requirements.

Опции представления

The Business Architectural Proof-of-Concept may take many forms, for example: 

  • a conceptual model of a solution using a notation such as UML
  • a simulation of a solution
  • a prototype
