From 1.0 to 2.0.1.1

The main change has been the addition of a new package providing guidance around the requirements flowdown approach. This is a top-down approach of logically decomposing the business systems across multiple aspects driven by the chosen architectural viewpoints and views. The externally visible behavior of the business (sub) systems is modeled using the concept of operations. For more information, see the  Define Business Operations Capability Pattern.
