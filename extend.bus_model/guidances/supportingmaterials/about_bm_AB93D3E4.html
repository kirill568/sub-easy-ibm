Content

This plug-in provides specific guidance on how to describe the "as-is" or the "to-be" business using a number of approaches and techniques at different levels of formalism. For a more detailed presentation, see the Business Modeling Discipline

Dependencies

This plug-in depends on the RUP plug-in. For "pure" business modeling projects, where the only goal is to deliver a model of the current "as-is" business and/or the "to-be" business, the RUP relationships could be ignored. In a software development project, the business modeling effort will be just one part of the overall effort, mostly concentrated in the Inception and Elaboration phases.
