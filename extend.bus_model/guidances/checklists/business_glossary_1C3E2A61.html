Элементы справочной таблицы
Does each term have a clear and concise definition
Is each glossary term included somewhere in the descriptions of the business use cases
If not, it may imply that a business use case is missing or that the existing business use cases are not complete. It is more likely, though, that the term is not included because it is not needed. In that case, you should remove it.
Are terms used consistently in the brief descriptions of business actors and business use cases
Does a term represent the same thing in all business use cases
