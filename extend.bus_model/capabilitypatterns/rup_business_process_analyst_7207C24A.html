Дескриптор роли: Business-Process Analyst
This role leads and coordinates business requirements elicitation by outlining and delimiting the organization being modeled.
На основе роли метода: Business-Process Analyst
Взаимосвязи
Выполняет:
  • Assess Target Organization
  • Capture a Common Business Vocabulary
  • Identify Business Goals and KPIs
  • Maintain Business Rules
  • Set and Adjust Objectives
Ответственный за:
  • Business Glossary
  • Business Goal
  • Business Rule
  • Business Use Case Model
  • Business Vision
  • Supplementary Business Specification
  • Target-Organization Assessment
Дополнительно выполняет:
  • Business Architectural Analysis
Изменяет:
  • Business Glossary
  • Business Goal
  • Business Rule
  • Business Vision
  • Target-Organization Assessment
